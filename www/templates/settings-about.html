Bankmybiz.com is a matching network of business owners and lenders. As an entrepreneur, time is your most precious commodity. We'll save you time and help get you the financing you need by informing you about which funding products you qualify for and then connect you directly with local and national lenders.

Relationship Recommendations

Bankmybiz is a Lending Relationship Network. Using key elements of your profile and the profiles of our network of lenders, we give you personalized Relationship Recommendations to introduce you to lenders that specialize in lending to businesses like yours.

A lender is more than just a source of funding. Lenders are super connectors and mavens inside of business networks. When you work together they have all the incentive to make introductions for you to potential customers, suppliers, and ancillary services such as accountants and lawyers.

The internet is a fast and easy way to make connections, build trust, and start and continue relationships. On Bankmybiz.com we provide a way to make connections fast and build trust and relationships from the comfort of your desktop or mobile phone.

Funding Option Recommendations

In addition to Relationship Recommendations we also leverage the details of your private and secure profile to give you Funding Option Recommendations. Bankmybiz.com recommends fundtion options that you most likely will qualify for. You can use Bankmybiz.com no matter what stage of business you are in. Just answer each question on your profile and you will immediately get suggestions for products and lenders.

It takes 5 minutes to complete your profile. We don't require overburdensome financials to get started with your profile. We match you on just a few fields including your industry, revenue size, employee size, business type, number of years a business has filed taxes, and location.

Questions And Advice

The home page of Bankmybiz.com is a stream of questions, advice, and milestones about business, financing, banking, and any other interesting or helpful thing that the network creates. Post challenges and advice about your industry and business to get advice (or feedback) and receive Bankmybiz Credit. The Bankmybiz.com network is where you can get advice and demonstrate your thought-leadership in your industry.

You control the solicitation of lenders on Bankmybiz.com. Businesses can contact lenders and other business owners, but you must put lenders in your pipeline or be a match for them to reach out to you. By asking questions and posting advice, you are building connections and demonstrating your expertise. Try it out today.

If you see a challenge you share or some advice that you think is right on the money, vote it up!. By voting questions, answers, and comments up the poster receives Bankmybiz Credit which shows their credibility and value to the Bankmybiz.com Network. On the other hand, if you see something wrong or not helpful, vote it down. This communicates that that user could be more helpful than their being.

Milestones

The goal is to raise money, to get the funding you need to grow your business, and when you succeed why not share your success!? Bankmybiz.com Milestones are a way to track and earn credit for your successes.

To initiate a Milestone from the homepage or from a user's profile click "New Milestone." From there select the user you reached your Milestone with, and select its ball-park size and put in a note about your goals and what your success feels like! Congrats!

The Relationship Pipeline

Use your Bankmybiz.com Pipeline to stay up to date with the activity of the lenders and business owners you want to learn from or do business with. By adding users to your Pipeline you will receive updates when they post questions and advice or reach milestones. Treat your Bankmybiz.com Pipeline like a CRM of your funding prospects.

You control the solicitations of lenders on Bankmybiz.com. If you put a lender into your Pipeline that gives them permission to contact you. So if you want to invite a lender to contact you, just add them to your Pipeline.
